# Find Your North Star

A guided, private way to discover your **Massive Transformational Purpose** — your North Star.

Two paths, one star:
- **The Workshop** — a 45–90 minute guided on-ramp to a first real draft.
- **Destiny Discovered** — the 30-question deep dive you live with over time.

**Private by design.** Everything you write is saved **only on your own device** — never sent to a server. Optionally lock it with a passphrase (encrypted on your machine), print it, or download a copy. Nothing leaves your computer unless you choose to share it.

Static HTML/CSS/JavaScript — no build step, no tracking, no accounts.
